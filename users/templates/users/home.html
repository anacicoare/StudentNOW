ABOUT US

StudentNOW is a website that helps you organize all of you college taxes. It has a build-in system of loyalty points that can be exchanged for promotions (caffees, food etc.), courses and even lessons held by fellow students. It also provides a friendly UI for you to pay your dorm and college taxes easier. Hope you enjoy it!
